# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain unit tests, integration tests, and technical documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work during sprint planning
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Collaborate with UX Designers on implementation feasibility
- Work with Data Analysts to instrument metrics and analytics
- Support Technical Writers with code examples and API documentation

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability
- Ensure implementation aligns with design specifications

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups facilitated by Scrum Master
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs and architecture decisions
- Collaboration with UX Designers on implementation details
- Code documentation reviews with Technical Writers

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics in collaboration with Data Analysts
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics
- Work with UX Designers to define user experience requirements
- Partner with Data Analysts to track feature adoption and impact
- Review and approve user-facing documentation with Technical Writers

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability
- Deliver features that meet user needs and business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs
- User research reviews with UX Designers
- Metric reviews and data analysis sessions with Data Analysts

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate or coordinate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives) with Scrum Master
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting with Technical Writers
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication
- Track project metrics and milestone progress with Data Analysts
- Manage resource allocation across all team roles

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders
- Ensure effective collaboration across all team roles

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation
- Collaboration with Scrum Master on sprint progress
- Documentation coordination with Technical Writers for project artifacts

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove impediments, and coach teams on Scrum practices. They ensure the team follows agreed processes and continuously improves.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate sprint planning, daily standups, reviews, and retrospectives
- Remove blockers and impediments to team progress
- Coach team members on Agile and Scrum best practices
- Shield the team from external distractions
- Track sprint metrics (velocity, burndown) and promote transparency
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and flow
- Ensure smooth sprint execution with minimal disruptions
- Build team self-organization and accountability

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and blocker resolution
- Sprint ceremony leadership (planning, review, retro)
- Coordination with Project Managers on cross-team dependencies
- Metrics reporting and improvement recommendations

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, design interfaces, and validate solutions to ensure products are intuitive and delightful. They bridge user requirements with technical implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Define user flows and interaction patterns
- Collaborate with Product Managers on feature requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation
- Establish and maintain design systems and style guides

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction scores
- Ensure design consistency across features

### Typical Communication
- Design review sessions with Product and Engineering
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Design specifications and interaction guidelines
- Collaboration on acceptance criteria with visual requirements

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts measure outcomes, analyze trends, and provide insights to inform product and business decisions. They ensure teams have the metrics needed to validate success.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Build dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Analyze user behavior and feature adoption
- Provide data-driven insights for prioritization
- Validate success metrics defined in project goals
- Support A/B testing and experimentation

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Measure and communicate product impact
- Identify opportunities through data analysis

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews with Product and Engineering
- Dashboard updates and trend analysis reports
- Ad-hoc data requests and analysis
- Success metric validation in retrospectives

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create and maintain documentation that helps users, developers, and stakeholders understand and use the product effectively. They ensure documentation is clear, accurate, and up-to-date.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API documentation, and tutorials
- Create release notes and changelog entries
- Document technical processes and runbooks
- Review and edit technical content for clarity
- Collaborate with Developers and Product on documentation requirements
- Maintain documentation standards and templates

### Goals
- Provide clear, accurate, and accessible documentation
- Reduce support burden through self-service resources
- Keep documentation synchronized with product changes

### Typical Communication
- Documentation review sessions with Engineering and Product
- Release notes coordination with Project Managers
- Content updates based on feature releases
- Documentation standards and template guidance
